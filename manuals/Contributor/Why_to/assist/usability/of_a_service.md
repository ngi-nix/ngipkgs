# Why to assist usability of a service?

To configure a working service, users should have a way
that does not require them to learn complex or boring
integrations into the services it requires or may be integrated into.

Users should remain in full control of enabling or disabling dependent services.
and only be guided to the relevant documentation to enable and configure them properly.
